### Hammerspoon

Hammerspoon is a tool for powerful automation of macOS.

It's been downloaded over **927,379 times**!

At its core, Hammerspoon is just a bridge between the operating system and a Lua scripting engine.

What gives Hammerspoon its power is a set of extensions that expose specific pieces of system functionality, to the user.

Hammerspoon is the "engine" behind CommandPost.

Chris is one of the active maintainers of the Hammerspoon project.

You can download and learn more on the [Hammerspoon website](https://www.hammerspoon.org).
