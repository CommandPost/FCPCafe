### SpliceKit

**Final Cut Pro, unlocked. A Command Palette, MCP server, and an open plugin framework to do almost anything.**

![](/static/splicekit-3-3-1.png)

**SpliceKit** is a free and open-source Mac application for control Final Cut Pro.

It was created by [Brian Elliott Tate](https://www.instagram.com/elliotttate/).

It's free and open-source and [lots of other people have contributed](https://github.com/elliotttate/SpliceKit/graphs/contributors).

The SpliceKit icon was designed by the amazing [Matthew Skiles](http://matthewskiles.com).

Chris at LateNite runs the website, moderates the Discord community and also makes code contributions.

You can learn more on the [SpliceKit website](https://splicekit.fcp.cafe).
